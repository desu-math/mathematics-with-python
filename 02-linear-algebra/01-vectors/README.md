# Vectors

> **Understanding vectors and their operations through mathematics and Python.**

## Introduction

Vectors are one of the fundamental concepts in Linear Algebra. They are used to represent quantities that have both **magnitude** and **direction**. Vectors play an essential role in mathematics, physics, engineering, computer graphics, and modern Data Science.

This chapter introduces the basic concepts of vectors and demonstrates how to perform vector operations using Python.



# Topics Covered

* Scalars and Vectors
* Vector Notation
* Magnitude (Norm)
* Direction
* Position Vectors
* Unit Vectors
* Equal, Zero, and Negative Vectors
* Vector Addition
* Vector Subtraction
* Scalar Multiplication
* Dot Product
* Cross Product
* Projection of Vectors
* Angle Between Vectors
